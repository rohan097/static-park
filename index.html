How does it even work ?


Voice

Your voice is recorded.

Feature Extraction

The data from your voice is analysed, and features are extracted.

Random Forest

A Random Forest Regression is used to predict where you are in the UPDRS scale.

Neural Network

A Feedforward Neural Network is used to classify whether you have Parkinson's Disease.
